Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please indicate this in the subject line of your message.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes, we offer technical support to all our users. Free plan users receive community support, while paid plans include direct email support. Business plan users also receive priority support with faster response times.

Can I request a demo?

Absolutely! We offer personalized demos for teams and businesses. Please fill out the contact form and select "Request a Demo" as the subject, and our sales team will reach out to schedule a time that works for you.

Do you have a help center or documentation?

Yes, we have a comprehensive help center with documentation, tutorials, and guides. You can access it at help.dartai.com. Our help center is regularly updated with new content to help you get the most out of Dart.
