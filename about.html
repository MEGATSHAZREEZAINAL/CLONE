About Dart

We're on a mission to revolutionize project management with AI, helping teams work smarter and achieve more.

Our Story

Dart was founded in 2023 by a team of engineers, designers, and product managers who were frustrated with the limitations of existing project management tools. We saw an opportunity to leverage the power of AI to create a more intelligent, intuitive, and efficient way to manage projects.

Our team has experience working at leading technology companies like Google, Microsoft, and Atlassian, and we've brought that expertise to Dart. We're passionate about creating tools that help teams work better together and achieve their goals.

Today, Dart is used by thousands of teams around the world, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. We're proud to be helping teams work smarter and achieve more with less effort.

Our Mission

We believe that AI has the power to transform how teams work together. Our mission is to create tools that harness this power to make project management more efficient, effective, and enjoyable.

Innovation

We're constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible with AI and project management.

Collaboration

We believe that great work happens when teams work together seamlessly.

Impact

We measure our success by the impact we have on our customers' productivity and success.
